Whole Wheat Pancakes

Ready in: 20 minutes
Ingredients: 8
Serves: 4

Nutrition Information

Calories: 220 per serving

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons oil

Directions

  1. Mix dry ingredients in one bowl.
  2. Whisk wet ingredients in another.
  3. Combine both, stir gently.
  4. Cook on a hot pan until golden on both sides.

Cooking Tips

  • Use a non-stick pan to avoid sticking.
  • Let the batter rest for 5 minutes before cooking.
  • Top with fresh fruits or a drizzle of honey.
